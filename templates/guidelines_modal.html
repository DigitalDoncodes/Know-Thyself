Resume Guidelines
Upload Rules
  1. Create a single PDF résumé (not Word or image).
  2. Upload within 48 hours of clicking “Apply”.
  3. File size must be ≤ 5 MB.
Mandatory Resume Contents
  • Name & Email
  • Professional summary
  • Education
  • Skills
  • Key achievements
  • Professional experience
  • Projects
  • Certificates & courses
  • Awards & recognition
  • Leadership & extracurriculars
  • Additional info (e.g. Languages)
Pro tip: Tailor your resume based on the job you want!
