Support Center

Find answers to common questions and get help with the platform.

Account & Registration

Click on "Sign Up" from the login page. Fill in your student ID, full name, email, phone number, and create a secure password. Your account will be created immediately and you can start using the portal.

Currently, password reset must be done manually. Please contact our support team at psychologyresumemail@gmail.com with your student ID and registered email for assistance.

Make sure you're entering your student ID exactly as you registered it. Student IDs are case-sensitive. If you're still having trouble, contact support with your registration details.

Job Application Process

Students can view open jobs on their dashboard and click the "Apply Now" button. You can only have one active application at a time to ensure focused job searching.

This policy ensures students give their full attention to each application and helps maintain quality applications. Once your current application is completed (approved/rejected), you can apply for another position.

You have 48 hours from the time you click "Apply Now" to upload your resume. After this deadline, your application will be automatically rejected.

Only PDF files are accepted for resume uploads. Please convert your resume to PDF format before uploading to ensure compatibility and formatting preservation. [Maximum File Upload Size 5MB]

Application Status & Timeline

Your application will be reviewed by the teacher once your resume is uploaded within 48 hours of applying. After the review process is complete, you will receive confirmation about your application status.

Log into your student dashboard to view your current application status. The status will show as "Pending Resume," "Submitted," "Approved," "Rejected," or "Corrections Needed."

This means the teacher has reviewed your application and requires some modifications or additional information. Check for feedback comments and contact support if you need clarification on what changes are needed.

Resume & Documents

Include your personal information, educational background, skills, any work experience or internships, projects, certifications, and relevant achievements. Keep it concise, well-formatted, and tailored to the job you're applying for.

The maximum file size allowed is 5MB. Most PDF resumes are well under this limit. If your file is too large, try compressing the PDF or reducing image quality if it contains photos.

If you've uploaded an incorrect resume or applied for the wrong position, please reach out to us immediately. Draft an email explaining your situation and send it to psychologyresumemail@gmail.com or dhatchinamoorthiai@gmail.com. Our team will review your request and help resolve the issue promptly.

Job-Related Questions

All available jobs are displayed on your student dashboard when you log in. Only "open" jobs with available vacancies are shown. New jobs are posted regularly by teachers and placement officers.

Due to the one-active-application policy, you can only apply for one position at a time, regardless of whether they're from the same company or different companies. Focus on the role that best matches your interests and qualifications.

Mobile & Accessibility

Yes! The portal is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and computers. All features including job browsing, application submission, and resume upload work seamlessly on mobile devices.

The portal works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, please ensure your browser is updated to the latest version.
