About Psychology Job Portal

Connecting students with opportunities, empowering futures.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and industry by providing a seamless, efficient, and transparent platform for students to find meaningful job opportunities. We empower teachers and placement officers with the tools they need to guide students toward successful careers.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every student has equal access to career opportunities and the resources to succeed. JobPortal aims to be the leading platform for campus recruitment, known for its integrity, innovation, and commitment to student success.

Meet the Creator

Dhatchina Moorthi TA

Developer

Dhatchina Moorthi TA is a Full-Stack Developer and Educational Technology Innovator working in the Psychology Department at Kumaraguru College. With expertise in Python, Flask, and modern web technologies, he specializes in creating comprehensive educational platforms that bridge the gap between psychology and technology. As the lead developer of the JobPortal system, he has revolutionized campus recruitment processes through innovative automation, intuitive user interfaces, and psychology-inspired design principles. His work focuses on creating meaningful career opportunities for students while streamlining administrative processes for educational institutions.
