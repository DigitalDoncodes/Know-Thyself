Application Guidelines
  1. Create a single PDF résumé.
  2. Upload within 48 hours of clicking “Apply”.
  3. Maximum file size 5 MB.
Resume Guidelines - Mandatory Contents

Please ensure your Resume contains the following mandatory sections:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Professional summary
  • Education
  • Skills
  • Key achievements
  • Professional Experience
  • Projects
  • Awards and recognition
  • Certificates and courses
  • Leadership and extracurricular
  • Additional information (e.g., Language)

Some extra information should be added according to the requirements of the job you apply for.

For more queries, contact the support centre.
